Access to Archaeological Data

Access archaeological or cultural data is very sensitive, to view or access this data please use Remote Access to Archaeological Data (RAAD). RAAD is an online GIS application that lets authorized users view and download spatial and tabular data about B.C.'s archaeological sites in a variety of formats.
